Meet the Coach

Grace Stewart

Grace Stewart is a former championship player and one of the most accomplished volleyball coaches in the Christian County area. A Murray State University graduate with over 12 years of experience spanning competitive play, head coaching, camp directing, and private instruction, Grace brings a deep understanding of what it takes to develop great players at every level.

"My focus is on the whole athlete — strengthening skills and the mind for big moment performance. I believe in promoting positive attitude and leadership qualities. Your athlete will learn what coaches are looking for and how to stand out from every other player on the court."

— Coach Grace Stewart · EliteVB · Hopkinsville, KY

Services & Pricing

What Coach Grace Offers

All sessions held at Heritage Christian Academy, Hopkinsville, KY. Open to players entering 6th grade or above with at least 1 year of experience. Sessions available in 1-hour or 90-minute formats.

🎯

Private Lessons

One-on-one sessions focused entirely on your player's technique, positioning, and individual goals. Maximum attention, maximum growth.

1-on-1 · All Positions $40/hr
👥

Small Group Lessons

Train with 2–4 players in a high-energy collaborative setting. Great for teammates, friends, or players at a similar skill level.

2–4 Players $30/hr per player
📈

Skill Development Sessions

Targeted training on specific skills — passing, serving, hitting mechanics, setting, blocking, footwork, or defensive technique.

Position-Specific $40/hr per player
🏐

Team Training

Customized sessions for full teams covering system work, serve receive, communication, game prep, and building a winning culture.

Full Teams Contact for Pricing
☀️

Half Day Camp

A focused 3-hour camp packed with skill-building drills, competition reps, and fundamentals in a fun, high-energy environment.

3 Hours · Youth $300
🌟

Full Day Camp

A full 6-hour camp covering every aspect of the game — serving, passing, hitting, defense, and team play from start to finish.

6 Hours · Youth $450
